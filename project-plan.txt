# Actions
1. Client contact or contacted
2. Sales Appointment is for Sales Specialist or Sales Technician to go on site for survey the job.
3. Customer provides FLOOR PLAN to the Sales man.
4. Sales Specialist or Sales Technician upload the plan with floor, description, details of customer.
5. From sales specialist report Project Designer or Collaborator will create project map with require devices and items.
6. Projects will be assigned to TECHNICIAN or INSTALLERS by Admin.
7. TECHNICIAN or INSTALLERS can updates progress of each items and can note information.
8. Customer can access their projects with information like progres, quote and invoice. but they can not edit.
